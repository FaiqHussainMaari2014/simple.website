Passage Writing

Ali was bored. Outside, the monsoon rain were Drumming on the roof and turning the Garden into a Lake.

Ali picked up his Mother's Tablet. He searched for a Holiday Island and a Picture of Hawaii came up.

Hawaiian men and women were sitting under trees and making Clothes .The Forest Seemed much nicer than

Ali's little garden ; he wished he could be there. The tree almost seemed to be moving in the Breeze
